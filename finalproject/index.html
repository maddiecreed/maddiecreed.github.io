A demographic exploration of Republican disunity over school voucher policy

An analysis of support for school vouchers by party and demographics at the state level. Data was collected from 7 states who proposed or passed school voucher legislation between 2022-2024.

We can further compare these demographics between states and one state versus all states. Consider Georgia, which has a different ordering of demographics compared to all states

In constrast to most states, in Georgia a higher black population tends to correlate with more votes against the bill. One potential reason is that the Black population's size provides more voting power for Black people to vote against the bill. Or, given the history of freedom schools in Georgia, Black Georgian's may be more likely to support private schools.
